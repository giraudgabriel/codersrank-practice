Breathe in, breathe out

Your next day will be better than today, but you will have to do your best to make it so. You will be doing something worthwile, so get ready!

General recommendations:

  • Don't forget that you deserve caring about yourself.
  • You are not starting/completing anything, so don't think "I can't do this right now", think "I'll rest / do something else for now" - that's a more positive, outward mindset.
  • Make the process as enjoyable as possible, ask yourself instead of forcing; you both are arguing for the same case, so search for a compromise.

Be honest with yourself

Calm down, don't get emotions and assumptions get in the way of judging yourself. Don't think about others, don't think about what you want to become. Be objective and compare yourself to yesterday's self.

If you did great one day, doesn't mean that you can or should do that every day. You deserve rest too. don't push yourself too hard, and when you do, don't take it as a baseline.

Be grateful to others. It's your job to appreciate them, and you should count on them when you need them. Be careful, but being alone and only counting on yourself is not an option.
